Safety Guidelines

Emergency Weather Preparedness

  • Keep a battery-powered or hand crank radio for emergency updates
  • Store at least 3 days worth of water and non-perishable food
  • Have flashlights and extra batteries readily available

Extreme Weather Actions

  • During thunderstorms: Stay indoors and avoid electrical appliances
  • In extreme heat: Stay in air-conditioned areas and avoid outdoor activities
  • During heavy snow: Avoid unnecessary travel and dress in layers
